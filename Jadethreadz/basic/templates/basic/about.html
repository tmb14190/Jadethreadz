About Jade

Jade has a natural creative flair when it comes to arts and crafts and has done since a small age. Particularly loving all things decoupage, fused glass and particularly sewing.

Studying psychology at Glasgow Caledonian university and a panel member for the Children’s hearing system. Jade loves to be busy, however any spare moment you will find her glued to her sewing machine.

Creation of Jadethreadz

March 2020… Global Pandemic… Lockdown…

Spending more time at home Jade started sewing all day, every day.

Jade created masks for her closest friends and family, having had such good feedback Jade decided to go live; selling face coverings on all social media platforms.
